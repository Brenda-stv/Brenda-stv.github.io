About Me

Hello, my name is Brenda Kong and I am a recent honors graduate from Arizona State Univeristy. I attended Barrett, the Honors College, and graduated with a bachelors in computer science in December 2023. I have been learning about how to code since I was a child, entirely taught by my father. I started with Python and eventually branched out to Java, C/C++, C#, SQL and JavaScript. I have always been a creative child who loved to draw and eventually, I found that these two passions blended together naturally over time. Soon I found myself engrossed in the idea of designing my own websites, as well as how I would go about designing when given a set of constraints or general themes to follow.
